Grocery Store Survey

Grocery Data Visualization:

Here we have used the Line Graph visualization technique. In this there are two types of visualization
1. Age v/s Purchase Amount
2. Age v/s Income
In both the cases the x axis demonstrates the Age and the y axis demonstrates the other variable
Through this graph we can conclude that the people between the age of 30-60 have a greater income while people almost have similar purchase amounts at every age.
Females have a more income and less purchase amount as compared to male
